Why I love Peaky Blinders so much?

Peaky Blinders has been with me through so many tough times. Back then, I was depressed, listless, and had lost all my fighting spirit. But watching the Shelby family strive and fight their way through everything made me feel like I wasn't alone. I'm truly grateful for the strength and encouragement this show has given me. I love it so much, and I highly recommend you watch it too!
